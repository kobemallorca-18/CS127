Cherry tomatoes are small, round-shaped, miniature version of traditional beefsteak tomatoes. These small tomatoes often have a sweeter taste than full sized tomatoes. It offers plenty of nutritions such as Vitamin C and Lycopene, an antioxidant that lowers the risk of cardiovascular diseases and cancer. Cherry tomatoes are usually used in salads, sauces, and soups.
