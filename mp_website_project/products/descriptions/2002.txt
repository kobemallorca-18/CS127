The Romaine lettuce has frilly tender, green leaves that are very popular in mixed salad greens. Their crisp leaves give a tasty flavor and also contain vitamin C. Romaine lettuce are one of the easiest to grow in a vegetable garden. To harvest, clip off the outer leaves at the base or just pull up the plant.
